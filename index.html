Route rules
  • Each city exploration hour is built from the clicks plotted on the map.
  • If the party changes pace, road type, or route leg mid-hour, change the floating control before the next click.
  • Main roads use the selected pace. Side roads / rubble forces slow pace for that movement.
  • Fast is 1 mile/hour, Normal is ½ mile/hour, and Slow is ¼ mile/hour. Side roads/rubble always uses the slow pace distance.
